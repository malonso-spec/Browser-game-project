How to Play

Welcome to a turn-based battle!

Each turn you pick a card to attack, defend or heal. The enemy strikes back automatically after your move.

Reduce the enemy's HP to 0 before yours runs out. Use your cards wisely!

★ Stunning Dance Damage: 20p

Stunning Dance

Your basic attack. Deals 20 damage to the enemy every turn.

This card is reusable — you can play it every turn without limit.

★ Rock Invocation Damage: 30p

Rock Invocation

A powerful attack that charges over time: 30 → 40 → 50 damage.

Using it resets the charge back to 30. Wait for maximum impact!

★ Bubble Gum Protection: 25p

Bubble Gum

Recovers 25 HP and activates a shield that blocks the next enemy attack to only 10 damage.

Single use — once played, it's gone for the rest of the battle.

★ Campero Boost +10HP · Cures drunk

Campero Boost

Recovers 10 HP, cures the Drunk status, and weakens the next enemy attack.

This card is reusable — use it every turn if you need healing.

Watch out! The enemy has special attacks:

⚡ Heavy — A devastating attack that deals massive damage. When you see the warning, brace yourself!

🍺 Drunk — The enemy can make you drunk, cutting your attack damage in half. Use Campero Boost to cure it!

Stay alert and use your cards strategically to survive.
